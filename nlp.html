Natural Language Processing: Text Frequency Analysis

For this project, I built a JavaScript program that acts as a basic text analyzer. The script takes a raw quote, cleans out punctuation, and skips common filler words like "the" or "and." By using a JavaScript Map to automatically track and count how many times each unique word shows up, the program generates a clean top-10 chart to show the most common terms in the research.

Source Text Selected for Analysis:

"Research over the past two decades broadly supports the claim that mindfulness meditation — practiced widely for the reduction of stress and promotion of health — exerts beneficial effects on physical and mental health, and cognitive performance. Recent neuroimaging studies have begun to uncover the brain areas and networks that mediate these positive effects."

Source Citation (MLA): Tang, Yi-Yuan, et al. "The Neuroscience of Mindfulness Meditation." Nature Reviews Neuroscience, vol. 16, no. 4, Apr. 2015, pp. 213-25. Nature Publishing Group, https://nature.com.
